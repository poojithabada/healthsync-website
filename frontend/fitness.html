Fitness

Find workout routines and fitness strategies to stay active and strong.

Beginner Workouts

If you're new to exercise, start with basic routines like walking, stretching, and light weightlifting. Consistency is key to building strength and endurance.

Strength Training

Strength training is important for muscle growth and overall health. Incorporate resistance exercises like weight lifting or bodyweight exercises into your routine.

Cardio for Heart Health

Cardiovascular exercises like running, cycling, or swimming are great for heart health. Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio each week.
